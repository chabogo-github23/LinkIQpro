Last Updated: [10th DEC 2025]

LinkIQpro values your privacy and ensures that all personal information is stored securely, used responsibly, and never shared without consent.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Client Information

  • Alias or username
  • Email (stored as hashed, not readable by Admins or Analysts)
  • Password (hashed)
  • Project submissions
  • Payment transaction data (from PayPal or Paystack)

1.2 Analyst & Admin Information

  • Only created by Super-Admin
  • Includes name, contact information, and performance records
  • Used strictly for workflow and platform operations

2. How Your Information Is Used

We use collected data to:

  • create and manage accounts,
  • facilitate project assignments,
  • process payments and escrows,
  • communicate project updates,
  • ensure platform security,
  • improve system performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

LinkIQpro implements industry-standard safeguards:

  • Emails and passwords are hashed and cannot be viewed by Admin or Analyst accounts.
  • Sensitive data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Access controls ensure only authorized staff and systems can process user data.
  • Logs, audit trails, and Username identifiers help maintain security and accountability.

4. Data Sharing

LinkIQpro does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Payment processors (PayPal, Paystack) for transactions,
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

No other sharing occurs.

5. Account Creation Rules

  • Clients self-register using email.
  • Admin and Analyst accounts are created ONLY by the Super-Admin to:
    • prevent fake accounts,
    • ensure Analysts are competent,
    • protect Clients from fraud,
    • maintain platform integrity.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for:

  • account functionality,
  • legal compliance,
  • dispute resolution,
  • system logging.

Clients may request account deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

Clients, Admins, and Analysts may:

  • request a copy of their stored data,
  • request correction of inaccurate information,
  • request deletion (unless prohibited by law),
  • withdraw consent where applicable.

To submit a request, contact [Insert Contact Email].

8. Cookies & Tracking

LinkIQpro uses cookies for:

  • session management,
  • security,
  • analytics,
  • user experience enhancements.

Users may disable cookies, but certain features may not function properly.

9. Third-Party Integrations

LinkIQpro integrates with:

  • PayPal
  • Paystack

These providers handle payment data externally and have their own privacy policies.

LinkIQpro does not store your full payment details.

10. Policy Updates

LinkIQpro may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
