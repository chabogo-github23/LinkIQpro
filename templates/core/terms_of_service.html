Definitions

Client
A registered user who submits projects, tracks progress, and makes payments through PayPal or Paystack.
Admin
A platform-appointed operator who manages projects, assigns Analysts, oversees milestones, and acts as the mediator between Client and Analyst.
Analyst
A vetted and platform-approved professional responsible for executing assigned projects.
Super-Admin
The top-level system controller who creates Admin and Analyst accounts, enforces compliance, and ensures platform integrity.
Escrow
A temporary holding mechanism where Client payments are securely stored until project or milestone completion.

Eligibility

  • Only Clients may self-register on ShadowIQ.
  • Admin and Analyst accounts are created exclusively by the Super-Admin to prevent fraudulent profiles and ensure expertise verification.
  • Users must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter into binding agreements.

Platform Overview

3.1 Client Responsibilities

  • Submit accurate project details and requirements.
  • Make payments through supported gateways (PayPal or Paystack).
  • Approve project completions and milestones in a timely manner.
  • Communicate respectfully through official platform channels only.

3.2 Admin Responsibilities

  • Assign each project to a qualified Analyst.
  • Oversee communication between Client and Analyst.
  • Create milestones where necessary.
  • Validate Analyst progress updates and mediate disputes.

3.3 Analyst Responsibilities

  • Deliver high-quality work according to specifications and deadlines.
  • Provide progress updates to Admin.
  • Maintain confidentiality and adhere to content guidelines.
  • Accept only projects assigned directly by the Admin.

Escrow and Payments

  • All Client payments are processed through PayPal or Paystack and placed in escrow.
  • Funds remain in escrow until:
    • the project is completed and approved by the Client, or
    • applicable milestones are completed and verified by the Admin.
  • After approval, payments are released:
    • Analyst receives their agreed payout.
    • Admin may receive service or administrative commissions (if applicable).
  • Refunds are issued only:
    • if the Client cancels before work begins,
    • if the Admin determines the Analyst failed to deliver,
    • or according to ShadowIQ's internal refund policy.

Project Workflow

  1. Client submits a project via the dashboard.
  2. Admin reviews the project and assigns it to the most competent Analyst.
  3. If needed, Admin creates project milestones.
  4. Analyst submits progress updates to the Admin.
  5. Client monitors progress via the platform.
  6. Completed work is submitted for Client review.
  7. Upon approval, escrow funds are released.
